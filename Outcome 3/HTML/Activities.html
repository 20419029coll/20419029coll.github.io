The Farm

Activities at The Farm

The farm park has many activities for people to do.

Some of these activities are hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze.

You can also:

  • visit the animals and see how they live in their natural habitat
  • play with farm tools, such as a pitch fork or shovel
  • pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you!

What To Do

Animal Feeding

Get up close with our friendly farm animals and help feed them during scheduled sessions.

Tractor Rides

Hop on board for a scenic ride around the farm and see the animals from a new perspective.

Indoor & Outdoor Play Areas

Let the kids burn off energy in our adventure playgrounds, both indoors and outdoors.

Meet Small Animals

Handle and meet our rabbits, guinea pigs, and other adorable small creatures.

Seasonal Events

We host fun seasonal activities throughout the year! Here are some of our popular events:

  • Pumpkin Picking (October)
  • Santa’s Grotto (December)
  • Easter Egg Trails (Spring)
  • Summer Fun Days (July–August)
