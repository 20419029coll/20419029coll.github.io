Frequently Asked Questions

The Farm is open 7 days a week from 9:30am to 5:00pm. Last entry is at 4:00pm, and some activities may close slightly earlier.

Booking online is highly recommended during weekends and school holidays. Tickets may be available on the day depending on visitor numbers.

Dogs (except certified assistance dogs) are not allowed due to the safety and welfare of our animals.
